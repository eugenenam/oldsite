Our Friends

At Honest Burgers, we take pride in working with and recognizing local farms in the Boston and New England region. Real Food. Real Farms. Real Communities. Here are some of our friends:

Lincoln Family Farms

The Lincoln Family Farm has been in business for over 3 generations. With over 120 acres of farmland, the Lincoln Family provides fresh beef from 100% grass-fed cows that graze on certified organic pastures. In addition, Lincoln farms has a certifed organic vegetable garden with Vegetables grown without pesticides and herbicides Vegetable availability is seasonal, but include lettuce, cucumbers, green beans, leeks, potatoes, kale, squash, carrots, turnips, garlic, onions, eggplants, and more.
